Gameplay

  1. Grid Layout:
    • The game presents a grid of a specific size, with a certain number of mines hidden within the grid.
    • The player's objective is to uncover squares that don't contain mines. Each square has an equal chance of containing a mine or being safe.
  2. Mine Count and Bets:
    • Players can choose the number of mines to be hidden in the grid. A higher number of mines generally means a higher payout, but also a greater risk.
    • Before starting the game, players place a bet. This bet is used to calculate the potential payout based on the number of safe squares uncovered and the number of mines.
  3. Uncovering Squares:
    • Players click on squares to reveal whether they contain a mine or are safe.
    • If the square contains a mine, the game ends, and the player loses the bet.
    • If the square is safe, the player can continue playing or cash out.
  4. Cashing Out:
    • At any point, players can choose to cash out and collect their winnings. The more squares they uncover without hitting a mine, the higher the payout.
    • Cashing out can occur after every safe square uncovered, allowing players to balance risk and reward.
