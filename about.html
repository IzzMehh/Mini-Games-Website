About this website


Welcome to PlayZz ! I'm Gauravv, the creator and developer behind this collection of fun and engaging mini-games. I started this website with a simple goal: to bring together a variety of interactive games that are easy to play and enjoyable for everyone.


Here, you'll find a selection of mini-games designed to test your skills and entertain you. Whether you're into classic games like dice and stone-paper-scissors or prefer modern twists like keno and mines, we've got something for everyone. Each game is designed to be intuitive and quick to play, perfect for a brief escape.


My passion for web development and user experience drives me to create a platform that's not only fun but also secure and user-friendly. I aim to continuously improve and expand the game collection, providing fresh experiences and exciting gameplay.


Thank you for visiting PlayZz. I hope you enjoy playing the games as much as I enjoyed creating them. If you have any feedback or suggestions, feel free to reach out to me through the Feed-Back Button. Let's play and have some fun!
