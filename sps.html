Gameplay

  1. Basic Rules:
    • Rock beats scissors.
    • Scissors beat paper.
    • Paper beats rock.
  2. Outcomes:
    • If both players choose the same shape, it's a tie, and the game is played again.
    • Otherwise, the winner is determined by the basic rules mentioned above.
Please note that winning in Stone-Paper-Scissors will not earn you PlayZz coins. This game is just for fun! Play as much as you'd like, but remember, no coins are awarded for wins here.
