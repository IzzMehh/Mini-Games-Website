Guess The Number

Gameplay

  1. How to play
    • The computer will randomly select a number from 0-1000 and the user have to find it out.

    • If the user find out the number in 15 try's he will be considered as winner and will get price on the basis of price distribution.

    • Computer will help the user to figure out the hidden number.

  2. Price Distribution
    • If the user find out the number under 5 try's he will get 2x of the amount he betted.

    • If the user find out the number after 5 try's he will get 1.5x of the amount he betted.

    • If the user didn't find out the number under 15 try's he will lose all the coin's he betted.
